About






Welcome to DESIGNX, the vibrant nexus where the realms of computer science and design converge, fostering a dynamic ecosystem of innovation and collaboration. Our symposium presents an extensive repertoire of tech and non-tech events, workshops, talks, and competitions, meticulously crafted to inspire and empower participants from diverse backgrounds. Immerse yourself in a kaleidoscope of experiences as you delve into the forefront of technology, amplify your skill set, and forge invaluable connections with industry luminaries. At DESIGNX, we invite you to embark on an exhilarating odyssey of creativity and exploration, where every idea is a catalyst for transformative change. Join us and become an integral part of a thriving community committed to sculpting the landscape of tomorrow's technology and design. Your voice, your vision, and your passion - together, we shape the future.
